FAQ

Common Questions

How does billing work?
All units are billed every 4 weeks at a flat rate. There are no hidden fees or surprise charges. The current promotion gives you the first 8 weeks at 50% off the regular price on any unit size. Contact us to discuss payment options when you reserve.
Is there a minimum rental period?
Contact us directly to discuss rental terms. We work with customers to find a setup that fits their timeline, whether that is short-term storage during a move or long-term ongoing rental.
What can I store in my unit?
Most household items, furniture, appliances, business inventory, seasonal items, tools, and recreational equipment like ATVs and motorcycles are welcome. Hazardous materials, flammable substances, perishable food, and illegal items are not permitted. Not sure about a specific item? Just call us.
How does 24/7 camera monitoring work?
Security cameras cover the facility continuously, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. This provides an ongoing record and acts as a strong deterrent. You have 24/7 access to your unit so you are never restricted by office hours.
Which size unit do I need?
A general guide: the 3'8"x10' and 4'x10' units suit boxes, seasonal items, and smaller household items. The 10'x10' fits a full one-bedroom apartment and is the most popular size. The 10'x20' is right for multi-bedroom homes, vehicles, or large equipment. If in doubt, call us and we will help you choose without pushing you to a larger unit than you need.
Is DOMTOR Storage locally owned?
Yes. DOMTOR Storage is owned and operated by a Cape Breton family based right here in Sydney, NS. We are part of DOMTOR Energy Services and Consulting, a locally rooted company that has been serving Cape Breton homeowners and businesses for years. When you rent from us, your money stays in Cape Breton.

Still have questions?

Call us directly. We are happy to help you figure out the right unit or walk you through the reservation process.
